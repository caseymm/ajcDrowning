ADOLESCENT DROWNING
In 2010, 3,782 people died from drowning.
One in five people who drown is age 14 or younger.
Leading Causes of Unintentional Injury-Related Death for Ages 1-14
Ages 1 - 14
For those ages 14 and younger, drowning remains the second-leading cause of unintentional injury-related death behind motor vehicle crashes and took 687 lives in 2010.
Ages 1 - 4
For children ages 1 to 4, drowning is the second-leading cause of death and took 436 lives in 2010.
